Film List

Hidden Figures

2016

Theodore Melfi tells the true story of three African American women mathematicians who contributed to the Apollo space mission, despite discrimination.

Radioactive

2019

The biopic of Marie Curie, the first woman to receive a Nobel Prize. A film about passion, ambition, and the sacrifices of a revolutionary scientist.

On the Basis of Sex

2018

The story of Ruth Bader Ginsburg and her fight for equal rights. An inspiring film about perseverance and justice.

Aux armes, citoyennes

ARTE Documentary

A documentary tracing the major female figures of the French Revolution and their fundamental role in political history.

Marie Curie

2016

A visual exploration of Marie Curie's journey and her revolutionary discovery of radioactivity.

Cherchez Natasha

Fiction Film

A dramatic comedy exploring the roles of women in traditionally male scientific and academic fields.
